Laboratory Work

Research Experience

🩹

HAP/Curcumin-Incorporated PVA/Alginate Wound Healing Patches

July 2025 – Present · With P. P. Mondal

Developed bio-active films incorporating hydroxyapatite (HAP) derived from Heteropneustes fossilis bones and curcumin extracted from turmeric root into a PVA/Alginate matrix. Conducted in-vitro drug release studies, antibacterial assays, and Brine Shrimp Lethality tests. Manuscript in preparation.

♻️

CNC-Reinforced PVA/Sodium Alginate Biodegradable Films

March – July 2025 · With P. P. Mondal

Developed high-performance biodegradable food packaging films via solvent casting, incorporating waste-paper-derived cellulose nanocrystals into a PVA/Na-Alginate matrix. Conducted XRD, FESEM, FTIR, TGA, mechanical testing, WVTR, WVP, and soil degradation tests.

⚗️

ZnO Thin Film Fabrication for Photocatalysis

July 2024 – March 2025 · With S. M. N. Rokon

Developed high-purity ZnO thin films via cost-effective Sol-Gel Spin Coating. Investigated synergistic effects of Ag/Rb and Cu/Rb co-doping to optimize photocatalytic degradation of Methylene Blue dye under light irradiation using XRD, FESEM, and UV-Vis Spectroscopy.
